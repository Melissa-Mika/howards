About Us

We are a family-owned bait and tackle shop located right in the heart of the ozarks. Our breadth of knowledge about fishing in various places allows us to confidently guide you in choosing the appropriate gear and bait for your fishing excursion. Let our experienced guides take you on a one-of-a-kind fishing excursion or just ask for their advice before heading out on your own. Our little slice of paradise has been in business for the past 30 years. Let us help you to have a fun and successful fishing experience!

Bait and Tackle Store

Our bait and tackle store has everything you need for your camping and fishing needs. We have camping supplies, including tents and lanterns. Firewood is for sale and is cut daily. We most likely carry anything you forgot to pack for your visit. Our fishing supplies are vast and include items such as fishing rods, spools, vests, coolers, and of course lots of types of live bait. Our staff are well versed on camping and fishing gear and are also available for guided fishing tours. You can obtain your fishing license here as well as have your rod and reel repaired. Our store also offers clothing should you have forgotten sweatshirts or dry weave clothing to keep you dry while out on the water.

 

Enjoy our Lake Big Fish

Lake Big Fish is the cornerstone of our slice of paradise. It is full of fish, including bass, walleye, and trout. Get your fishing gear from our store and be prepared to have a blast fishing from the shore or out on the lake. The lake is 2 miles long and 1 mile wide, a perfect size to explore. Our lake was voted "best lake to fish" for 3 years in a row.

 

We have kayak and canoe rentals too!

The fishing is great off of the shore, but we also have our lake to explore. If you would like to be out on the water, you can rent one of our kayaks or canoes and paddle to the other side of the lake, where we also have areas for picnics and swimming. There are also great areas along the shoreline for fishing.

 

Check out our Campground

Enjoy camping at our campground offering 35 tent sites. 12 RV sites with electric and sewer, and 2 cabins, which are fully furnished with a kitchen. Each campsite has a place to build your campfire and firewood is sold at the bait shop. There is a picnic table at each site as well, making a great place to eat the fish that you caught! Restrooms and showers are centrally located and the campground is well lit when walking around after dark.
